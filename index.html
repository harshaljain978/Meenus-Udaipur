The journey of meenu and her Family. from 1995 to 2026

1995: The Genesis

Meenu and her family start the legendary Queen's Cafe in Udaipur. Famed for its Pumpkin Curry, Banana Curry, and Kashmiri Pulao.

2007: Sharing the Art

Meenu's Cooking Classes began, teaching the true foundation of Indian spices and home-style techniques.

2010 - 2020: The Hiatus

The restaurant doors closed, but the spirit stayed alive through intimate food gatherings and constant passion.

2015: Culture Walks

Started Authentic Walking Tours—focusing not just on stones and history, but on the lifestyle and culture of the locals.

2024-2026: The Vision

Relaunched with Homy Rooms and Local Food Tours. A complete immersion into Udaipur's soul for the new era.
