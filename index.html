The Journey of
Meenu & Her Family

1995: The Foundation

Meenu and her mother opened the iconic Queen's Cafe, quickly becoming a sanctuary for travelers seeking the authentic soul of Udaipur's flavors.

2007: Cultural Exchange

The birth of Meenu’s Cooking Classes, an initiative to share the intricate secrets of Indian spices with the global community.

2015: Hidden Alleys

Walking Culture Tours began, inviting guests to step beyond palaces and into the living heart of our historic city.

2026: A New Era

Relaunching with expanded hospitality—offering bespoke Homy Rooms and dedicated Local Food Tours under the Meenu's name.
